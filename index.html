Prompting Framework

  • Subject(s): Who or what is central?
  • Setting & Environment: Time of day, location, weather.
  • Mood & Atmosphere: Emotional tone, color palette.
  • Composition & Framing: Camera angle, depth of field.
  • Lighting & Color: Light quality, color temperature.
