Prompting Framework

  • Subject(s): Who or what is central? Defined with precision and visual hierarchy.
  • Setting & Environment: Time of day, weather, location, and spatial context.
  • Mood & Atmosphere: Emotional tone, color palette, and visual energy.
  • Composition & Framing: Camera angle, depth of field, visual balance.
  • Lighting & Color: Light source quality, highlights, and temperature control.
  • Artistic Style & Medium: Choose or default to photorealism, cinematic, hand-drawn, or painterly.
  • Technical Specs: Resolution, aspect ratio, lens type, and fine detail—all tuned for impact.

Bonus: Creative defaults include volumetric lighting, ambient texture effects, and “hyper-detailed” realism—so your images look premium even when your prompt is minimal.
